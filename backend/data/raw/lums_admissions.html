Admissions

LUMS is committed to actively seeking out and developing brilliant students from diverse socio-economic backgrounds. Admission to LUMS is merit-based. Our community comprises people with varied talents, interests, backgrounds, and cultures. We prepare our scholars for a lifetime of learning and equip them to lead reform in their respective industries, families, and communities.

Learn more about the courses we offer, and find out how to make an application to join us.
