Mandatory Test Requirements

The HEC Undergraduate Studies Admission Test (USAT) is compulsory for admission in all BS and Pharm-D programs.

For LLB applicants, the HEC Law Admission Test (LAT) is strictly required. No application will be processed without a valid result.
