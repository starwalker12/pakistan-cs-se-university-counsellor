        • Introduction

          Quaid-i-Azam University (once named Islamabad University) was established in July 1967 under the Act of National Assembly and started teaching and research programmes for PhD and MPhil degrees. It was, however, gradually and later decided to offer Master’s, graduate, and now undergraduate programmes. The University, for its international repute, faculty and programmes attracts a large number of foreign students although it offers admission to a measured number of students from all regions of the country as it is a federal public sector university.
          It has been able to establish its links with some selected universities in Europe, South Asia and the United States. The international educational and research institutions including UNESCO, IRSIP, Agencia Espanole de Cooperacion International (Spain) etc. collaborate their research activities with the Quaid-i-Azam University.

Admission Requirements :

  1. A person holding HSSC Part-I passed with at least 50% marks result may apply for admission to Undergraduate/ BS (Morning/Evening) Programmes.
  2. For Pharm-D Programme FSc (Pre-Medical) or Equivalent Certificate with at least 60% marks.

  3. For LLB Programme

    1. Admission to the LLB (4-Year) program is subject to the submission of a valid LAT (Law Admission Test) result conducted by the Higher Education Commission (HEC).
    2. Admission to the LLB program will be determined based on a merit list calculated using the following formula:
      1. Higher Secondary School certificate (HSSC) Part-I or Equivalent: 50%
      2. Higher Education Commision’s Law Admission Test (LAT): 30%
      3. Secondary School Certificate (SSC): 20%

Schedule

Morning :

Admission process starts usually in the Month of June for Undergraduate Programme (Morning) once in a calendar year for (Fall Semesters).

Self Support :

Admission process starts usually in the Month of June for Undergraduate Programme (Self Support) once in a calendar year for (Fall Semesters).

How to Apply

  1. The applicants can apply through Online Application System at QAU website (qau.edu.pk). The applicant is advised to deposit Rs. 2860/- as application processing fee via online payment through Kuickpay or 1-Bill.
  2. The payment receipt must be uploaded on the online Admission form along with other scanned requisite documents; otherwise application will not be entertained.
